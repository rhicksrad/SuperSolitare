# Music credits

All tracks are public domain, originally distributed by FreePD.com (now offline,
mirrored at archive.org/details/freepd). No attribution is required, but credit
where it's due. Composers are taken from each file's ID3 tags; a few files
shipped untagged and are listed as uncredited.

Every round has its own track: `a{ante}-{small|big|boss}.mp3`. Endless antes
past 8 cycle through the ante 6–8 set. `menu.mp3` covers the menu, blind
select, and shop. Files are re-encoded to VBR MP3 (~130 kbps) for web delivery;
sound effects remain fully synthesized in `src/ui/audio.ts`.
